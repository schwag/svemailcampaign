Breast Cancer—Know your risk

There are a million reasons to see your doctor more often.

Determining your risk of breast cancer is just one of them. Even if you have no signs or symptoms of the disease, a mammogram can help detect breast cancer in its earliest stages.

By scheduling a visit with your doctor, you can review your family history and risk factors to determine if a mammogram screening is right for you.

Do yourself and your loved ones a favor. Schedule a well-visit with your doctor today.
